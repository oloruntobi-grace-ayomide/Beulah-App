About us

Beulah Foundation for Christ is not an established Church but a work and a part of the Body of Christ.

It is a Ministry geared towards preparing the man totally for the Coming of Jesus Christ our Lord and Savior.

It is a Ministry created and founded by God to disciple men in order for them to truly showcase His attitude and character in every facet of their lives.

This preparation entails evangelism and discipling as contained in Matthew 28:18-20.

The mandate of the Ministry is derived from Isaiah 62 verse 4.

The Ministry is therefore positioned to be a resource in all areas of discipline for the growth of the Body of Christ and the well being of the Society.

That's just a glimpse of who we are! Join us to discover the full story and become part of our joyful family in Christ. We can't wait to welcome you!
